About Me

Front End Web Developer who loves solving problems through programming and team collaboration.

Programming has been a part of my life since first coding on a Commodore 64 as a teen.  I moved on to learn Visual Basic by coding a graphical Scrabble game to play-by-email with family and friends.

I have been working with SQL, relational databases and Unix throughout my professional career.  Now I am excited to be learning Full Stack Web Development.

In my free time my two teenage daughters, wife and I enjoy camping on the Washington and Oregon coasts. I also enjoy reading world and military history, Astronomy (both night sky and solar observing) and watching the Seahawks.
